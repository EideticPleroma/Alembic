# Minor Arcana Reference

## Overview

The 56 Minor Arcana represent the everyday experiences of life, organized into four suits corresponding to the elements and aspects of human experience.

## The Suits

| Suit | Element | Domain | Season | Direction |
|------|---------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Wands** | Fire | Creativity, passion, will, career | Spring | South |
| **Cups** | Water | Emotions, relationships, intuition | Summer | West |
| **Swords** | Air | Thoughts, conflict, truth, decisions | Autumn | East |
| **Pentacles** | Earth | Material world, work, body, finances | Winter | North |

## The Numbers

Each number carries meaning across all suits:

| Number | Theme |
|--------|-------|
| **Ace** | Pure potential, seed, beginning |
| **Two** | Duality, balance, partnership, choice |
| **Three** | Creation, growth, collaboration, expression |
| **Four** | Structure, stability, foundation, rest |
| **Five** | Conflict, challenge, instability, change |
| **Six** | Harmony, communication, resolution |
| **Seven** | Reflection, assessment, inner work |
| **Eight** | Movement, power, mastery, regeneration |
| **Nine** | Near completion, integration, intensity |
| **Ten** | Completion, culmination, new cycle begins |

## Court Cards

| Card | Stage | Approach |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Page** | Student | Learning, messages, new energy |
| **Knight** | Quester | Action, pursuit, excess of element |
| **Queen** | Master (inward) | Mature expression, nurturing, receptive |
| **King** | Master (outward) | Authority, mastery, directing, active |

## Wands (Fire)

### Ace of Wands
**Upright**: Inspiration, new beginnings, potential, creative spark
**Reversed**: Delays, blocked creativity, lack of direction

**Upright**: Planning, future vision, decisions, discovery
**Reversed**: Fear of change, lack of planning, playing it safe

### Three of Wands
**Upright**: Expansion, foresight, overseas opportunities, progress
**Reversed**: Obstacles, delays, frustration, lack of foresight

### Four of Wands
**Upright**: Celebration, harmony, homecoming, community
**Reversed**: Lack of support, transience, home conflicts

### Five of Wands
**Upright**: Competition, conflict, rivalry, diversity
**Reversed**: Avoiding conflict, inner conflict, resolution

### Six of Wands
**Upright**: Victory, recognition, success, progress
**Reversed**: Egotism, fall from grace, lack of recognition

### Seven of Wands
**Upright**: Challenge, competition, perseverance, defense
**Reversed**: Exhaustion, giving up, overwhelmed

### Eight of Wands
**Upright**: Movement, speed, progress, swift action
**Reversed**: Delays, frustration, waiting, scattered energy

### Nine of Wands
**Upright**: Resilience, persistence, last stand, courage
**Reversed**: Exhaustion, paranoia, defensive, giving up

### Ten of Wands
**Upright**: Burden, responsibility, hard work, achievement
**Reversed**: Burnout, delegation, release of burdens

### Page of Wands
**Upright**: Enthusiasm, exploration, discovery, free spirit
**Reversed**: Setbacks, lack of direction, procrastination

### Knight of Wands
**Upright**: Energy, passion, adventure, impulsiveness
**Reversed**: Haste, scattered energy, delays, frustration

### Queen of Wands
**Upright**: Courage, confidence, independence, warmth
**Reversed**: Demanding, jealousy, selfishness, insecurity

### King of Wands
**Upright**: Leadership, vision, honor, entrepreneurship
**Reversed**: Impulsiveness, haste, ruthlessness, overbearing
