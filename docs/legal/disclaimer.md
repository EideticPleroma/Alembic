# Tarot Reading Disclaimer

## Purpose Statement

Alembic provides tarot readings powered by artificial intelligence for **entertainment, self-reflection, and personal insight purposes only**.

## What Tarot Readings Are

Tarot readings offered by Alembic are:

- **Reflective tools** for self-exploration
- **Archetypal mirrors** illuminating patterns in your life
- **Conversation starters** for personal growth
- **Entertainment** with psychological depth
- **Frameworks** for contemplation and introspection

## What Tarot Readings Are NOT

Tarot readings should **NOT** be considered as:

### Medical or Mental Health Advice
Readings are not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or psychiatric care. If you are experiencing mental health difficulties, suicidal thoughts, or emotional distress, please contact a qualified healthcare professional or crisis helpline immediately.

**Crisis Resources:**
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 (US)
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- International Association for Suicide Prevention: https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

### Financial or Investment Advice
Readings should not inform financial decisions, investments, or business strategies. Consult a qualified financial advisor for financial matters.

### Legal Advice
Readings are not legal counsel and should not influence legal decisions. Consult a qualified attorney for legal matters.

### Relationship Decisions
While readings may offer perspective on relationships, they should not be the sole basis for major relationship decisions. Consider professional counseling for significant relationship concerns.

## No Predictions

**Alembic does not predict the future.**

The cards and their interpretations reflect:
- Archetypal patterns and psychological dynamics
- Current energies and potential trajectories
- Themes for contemplation

They do not:
- Foretell specific events
- Guarantee outcomes
- Replace your own judgment and free will

## AI-Generated Content

Interpretations are generated by artificial intelligence and:
- May not perfectly match traditional tarot meanings
- Reflect the AI's training, not mystical insight
- Should be viewed as one perspective, not absolute truth
- May occasionally produce unexpected or imperfect responses
