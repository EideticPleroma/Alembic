# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

Alembic ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our tarot reading application.

## Data Controller

Alembic is the data controller responsible for your personal data.

**Contact**: privacy@alembic.app

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)

### Reading Data
- Questions you ask during readings
- Cards drawn and interpretations generated
- Follow-up conversations about readings

### Usage Data
- Reading history and preferences
- Feature usage patterns
- Session information

### Payment Data
- Processed by Stripe (we never see full card numbers)
- Subscription status and history
- Transaction records

### Technical Data
- IP address (for security and rate limiting)
- Browser type and version
- Device information

## Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

### Access Your Data
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### Correct Your Data
Update or correct inaccurate information.

### Delete Your Data
Request deletion of your account and associated data.

### Export Your Data
Receive your data in a portable format (JSON).

### Object to Processing
Opt out of certain types of processing.

### Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

### Exercise Your Rights
Contact us at privacy@alembic.app or use the in-app settings.

We may disclose data when required by law or to protect our rights.
